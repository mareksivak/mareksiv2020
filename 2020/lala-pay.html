Making better payments

Before and after

Lalamove system relied on 3rd party gateway to process paymentys. This negatively affected customer experience and fragmented user flows. Payments were tedious and processed on external pages. This provided limited visibility and control into experience of users for Product Team.

Building own capabilities enabled us to improve and personalise payment experience to customers by and directly handling the most popular payment methods for cashless payments by our system.

I worked on project aiming to seamlessly integrate payment methods into experience. I decided to use popup components which are used to gather another types of input from user at different parts of the app.
